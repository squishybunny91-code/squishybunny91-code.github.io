The Harshira are the nine most powerful Demon Slayers at Demon Slayer Corps.Their job is to protect humanity from deomns, train lower level Demon Slayers and serve as their mentors. Each of the Hashiras have different breathing style techniques, like in the Last Airbender. Some of them are mastered by majority of the Hashiras and other demon slayers or have unique styles that only a few of the use. There are about 13 different breathing styles which have envolved from the main 5 breathing styles, Flame, Water, Wind, Stone, and Thunder these include:

  1. Water Breathing

  2. Flame Breathing

  3. Thunder Breathing

  4. Beast Breathing

  5. Sun Breathing

  6. Insect Breathing

  7. Sound Breathing

  8. Mist Breathing

  9. Love Breathing

  10. Wind Breathing

  11. Serpent Breathing

  12. Stone Breathing

  13. Flower Breathing
