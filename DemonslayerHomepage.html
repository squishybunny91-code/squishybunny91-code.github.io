Demon Slayer is a orignally a Japanese manga written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. The story follows an older brother named Tanjiro Kamado who joins the Demon Slayer Corps to kill the demon who killed his family and turned his younger sister, Nezuko Kamado, into a demon. The mangas were in a weekly manga magazine line called the Weekly Shonen Jump, from Feburary 15th, 2016 to May 18th, 2020. There are 23 indiviual volumes.
