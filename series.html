Series

On April 6th, 2019 Demon Slayer was produced by Ufotable and released the first season with 26 episodes. Demon Slayer is also an anime series avalible on netflix, Hulu, and CrunchyRoll. There are currently five series avalible to watch:

  • Demon Slayer: Tanjiro Kamado, Unwavering Resolve Arc
  • Demon Slayer: Mugen Train Arc
  • Demon Slayer: Entertainment District Arc
  • Demon Slayer: Swordsmith Village Arc
  • Demon Slayer: Hashira Trainng Arc
There is also a new Demon Slayer movie that came out, Demon Slayer: Infinity Castle. It was released in Japan on July 18th, 2025. It was released in US and Canadian movie theators on September 12th, 2025. Currently, it is not available on any streaming services.
