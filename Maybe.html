MAYBE. by Sienna Spiro

Sienna Spiro is a 20-year-old singer-songwriter from London. She first started singing on TikTok and started realseing her own music in 2024. Sienna's music is described to be a mixture of jazz and soul, and she is compared to artists like Amy Whinehouse and Adele.


MAYBE. is the fifth song on Sienna's album SINK NOW, SWIM LATER. Which was released in 2025.
