Shiba by Olga Myko

Olga Myko is a Swedish-Ukrainian singer and songwriter. Her music is a described as hyper-pop and has unique beats, she first went viral on TikTok for her song "use me!!!!!!!"


Shiba is the third song on Olga's album WHEN A GIRL CRIES. Which was released in 2025.
