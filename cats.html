Cats & Pigeons

Cats

Cats are independent, curious, and agile animals. They have been domesticated for thousands of years.

Pigeons

Pigeons are intelligent birds known for their excellent navigation skills. Pigeons are native to parts of North Africa, Europe and the Medtrieana. They were also used during World War I and World War II, as messenager pigeons.
